Welcome to Dori's Cabinet of Plant Wonders and Oddities


This is my virtual cabinet of curiosities where I showcase my collection of unique and unusual plants. Each plant has a section with detailed information about its care, origins, and some fascinating and quirky facts associated with it. I hope you enjoy my tiny cabinet of plant wonders and curiosities.

About me:

Hi there! My name is Dori and I have been a collector of all things odd since my childhood. My collections consists of antiques, skulls and bones, insects, crystals and plants. In this tiny corner of the internet, I want to share with you my treasures, some plant profiles, care tips, DIY projects and inspire you to take a look at the fascinating world of the cabinets of curiosities.
